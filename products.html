FAQ.

Frequently asked questions about our foam products.

What sizes of mattresses do you produce?

We produce mattresses in all standard sizes including single (75"x36"), medium (75"x48"), queen (75"x54") and king (75"x72"). Custom sizes are also available on request.

Do you supply to distributors and dealers?

Yes. We distribute our products to sub-distributors and dealers across Nigeria from our production facility in Gauraka Suleja, Niger State. Contact us for bulk and dealer pricing.

Are your products SON certified?

Yes. All our foam products meet the required standards set by the Standard Organisation of Nigeria (SON), ensuring quality, safety and durability for every customer.
