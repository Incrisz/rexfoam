We're a foam producing company based in Gauraka Suleja, Niger State.

About Us

Our Story.

REX Foam Industries was established to meet household needs in furniture and comfort. We produce standard foam products including mattresses, pillows, mats and foam blocks, distributing to sub-distributors and dealers across Nigeria. Our production and office are located at Gauraka Suleja, Niger State. We are driven by creativity and innovation, with dedicated personnel committed to our core vision of delivering quality and satisfaction.

Our Products & Services

We deliver quality foam solutions for homes, offices and institutions across Nigeria.

Foam Mattress

Custom Foam

Office Furniture

We consult and supply office furniture for comfort purposes, handling every engagement with utmost expertise to achieve the client's set goals.

SON-Certified Quality Standards

All our foam products meet the standards set by the Standard Organisation of Nigeria (SON), ensuring every product is safe, durable and fit for purpose.

Wide Product Range for All Needs

From mattresses in all sizes to foam blocks, pillows, mats, duvets and upholstery foam — we cover every foam need for homes, hotels and offices.

Nationwide Distribution Network

From our production facility in Gauraka Suleja, Niger State, we distribute our products to sub-distributors and dealers across Nigeria.
